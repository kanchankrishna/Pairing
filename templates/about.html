GOAL: Bridge the gap between the international and domestic students and facilitate more diverse interactions.

Quarry Lane is unique in the sense that it equally comprises of both international and domestic students. However, there is an inherent gap between the two groups due to a lack of assimilation. This application is meant to bridge the gap through having any student, new or already-attending, enter their personal interests. My app will match them with someone who shares at least one thing in common.

Because just one commonality can spark wonderful conversations.
